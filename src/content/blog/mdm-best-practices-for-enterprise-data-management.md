In the modern landscape of data-driven organizations, proper governance, and maintenance of accurate, consistent, and reliable [data](/the-role-of-data-cataloging-in-enterprise-data-management/) are key. [Master Data Management](/master-data-management/) (MDM) frameworks offer a systematic way of managing the important data of an organization, ensuring accuracy, consistency, and availability across the whole organization. Following MDM best practices can provide better decision-making, more streamlined processes, and improved customer interactions when implemented properly. This exposition explores the paramount practices for implementing MDM frameworks, aiding organizations in maximizing their data’s potential. For a broader perspective, see our guide on what is [what is data governance](/what-is-data-governance-and-why-do-we-need-it/).
