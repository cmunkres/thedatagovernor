## Automated Data Classification

One of the key challenges in data governance is identifying and classifying data to determine its sensitivity and security requirements.

AI can be used to automate this process by analyzing the content of [data and classifying it according to pre-defined policies](/laying-the-foundation-for-data-governance-success-pillar-1-policies-standards/). This can reduce the time and effort required to organize data, improve consistency and accuracy, and reduce the [risk of data](/using-data-for-risk-management/) breaches.

## Intelligent Data Access Control

AI can also be used to intelligently control access to [data based on user roles](/unlocking-the-power-of-data-governance-the-role-of-a-data-steward/) and permissions. By analyzing user behavior and access patterns, AI can identify potential security threats and take appropriate actions, such as blocking access or flagging anomalous behavior. This can help improve [data security and reduce the risk](/using-data-for-risk-management/) of unauthorized access or data breaches.

## Automated Data Quality Checks

Data quality is critical for effective data governance. AI can be used to automate data quality checks and identify errors or inconsistencies in data. By flagging data quality issues in real time, AI can help ensure that data is accurate, consistent, and up-to-date, which is essential for effective decision-making.

## Predictive Analytics

AI can also be used for predictive analytics, which can help identify potential data governance risks and opportunities. By analyzing [data trends](/navigating-the-data-engineer-roadmap-2023-the-skills-and-trends-you-need-to-know/) and patterns, AI can identify potential risks, such as data breaches or compliance violations, and provide early warnings to data governance teams. It can also identify opportunities to improve [data governance,](/the-data-stewardship-pillar-the-2nd-data-governance-pillar/) such as identifying areas where data quality can be improved or where access controls can be tightened.

## Natural Language Processing

AI can also be used for natural language processing (NLP), which can help improve data governance by making it easier to search for and understand data. NLP can be used to extract meaning from unstructured data, such as emails or customer feedback, and categorize it according to pre-defined policies. This can improve data accuracy and make it easier for data governance teams to find and analyze data.

## Conclusion

Looking ahead, we can expect AI to continue to transform data governance in ways we can't even imagine yet. As data volumes grow and new data sources emerge, we will need increasingly sophisticated tools to manage and secure our data.

AI can provide those tools, helping us stay ahead of the curve and ensure that our [data is managed](/how-to-create-efficient-master-data-management-workflows-and-approvals/) effectively and securely.
