## Introduction: What is Data Lineage?

Data lineage is a concept that refers to tracking and tracing data used within an organization from its original source. It includes both the logical (data definitions and processing) and physical (data infrastructure) aspects of how data moves through an organization. 

By understanding this process, organizations are able to identify quality issues with raw data before it reaches consumption or end-user applications. The tracking of data lineage from initial capture through all processes, storage formats, locations, transformations, and ownership stages gives organizations insight into their entire data management environment.

## Role of Data Lineage in Data Governance and the Enterprise

[Data governance](https://amzn.to/3HpRvtU) is essential for ensuring that an organization's operations run smoothly by managing access control over information assets. In order to do this successfully, organizations must have a good understanding of where their [data comes from and how it moves through different](/data-engineer-vs-data-scientist-the-dynamic-duo-of-data-management-and-analysis/) components or systems within the company's infrastructure. 

This means tracking physical flows alongside logical ones so that any discrepancies or anomalies can be easily identified early on in the process. With proper oversight over these flows, organizations can ensure that only clean and accurate data makes its way into their applications or analytics programs.

Data lineage also plays a key role in helping organizations comply with regulations such as GDPR or HIPAA which require that they have visibility into exactly who has access to what kind of personal information at all times. 

By being able to trace back each piece of information within an organization’s databases or archives all the way up to its original source, companies can more easily monitor who has permission to use certain types of customer information and monitor for any unauthorized access attempts more quickly than ever before.

## Benefits of Leveraging Data Lineage for an Organization

By leveraging insights derived from tracking their data lineage throughout their entire business environment, organizations are able to transform their approach towards better governance over their information assets while simultaneously reducing the risk associated with potential security breaches or compliance violations associated with mishandling customer data. 

Here are some ways in which leveraging this powerful insight tool helps your business:

* Improved Quality Control: Identify quality issues with raw input earlier on in your pipeline so you can detect errors before they become costly mistakes down the line;
* Increased Compliance and Security: Traceback every piece of customer information up to its original source so you can spot any discrepancies or unauthorized access attempts quickly;
* Better Debugging Capabilities: Isolate errors faster during the debugging process by pinpointing exact issue areas quickly;
* Streamlined Business Processes: Automate certain steps during your workflow using insights obtained from your data lineage so you don't have to manually manage everything;
